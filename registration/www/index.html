5 Steps of Punia

The Five Stages of Digital Transformation to SMEs goes to class.

1. Know Yourself

At this stage, SME entrepreneurs need to know themselves and their businesses, such as the type of business, the legality required, the business partners, the source of raw materials, the marketing area, and others. This information will form the basic profile of SMEs.

2. Record the Data

The next step is to record every business-related static data, such as a list of goods, a list of prices, a list of customers, a list of suppliers, and a list of employees. This static data will be useful in recording transactions at a later stage.

3. Record Transactions

Complete the recording, at this stage carried out recording transactions such as purchase transactions, sales, inventory counting, employee payroll, and so forth. Recording of transactions is done in stages in accordance with the needs and abilities of SMEs.

4. Business Process

If static data and transactions are well-documented, SME management can be improved by implementing a structured and systematic business process. The important thing in the implementation of business processes is discipline and consistent in running and taking note.

5. Reports & Analysis

The last stage is to make operational and financial reports. Using these reports SME players can conduct business analyzes that will further support the development and progress of their business.
